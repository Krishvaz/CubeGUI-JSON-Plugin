Your answers will **not** be transmitted or stored!

There are two types of questions:

### q:{Example: single choice}

The number 2 is *even*.

- [X] Yes
- [ ] No

### :end:

If multiple alternatives are selectable, several of them may be correct. Press the *button* to reveal the answers. For each correct selection, the checkbox will turn green; for each incorrect selection, it will turn red and the selection will also be highlighted in yellow.

### q:{Example: multiple choice}

The number 6 is *divisible* by

- [X] 2
- [X] 3
- [ ] 4

### :end:

Please note that all answers may include additional explanations and hints. So itâ€™s definitely worth reviewing both the correct and incorrect answers.
